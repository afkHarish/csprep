About the website

Hey everyone, I am Harish aka the developer of CSPrep. This idea of having a one stop destination for all the necessary resources under a website striked me when many of my friends told me about their bad experiences of finding helpful websites for them to get started with preparation. That moment i decided to solve this crisis, and then CSPrep was born. This website is totally FOR THE STUDENTS, OF THE STUDENTS, and BY A STUDENT. Hope this website serves as a good resource for you all. Good luck for your preparation and rooting that you get for your first job with ease!
